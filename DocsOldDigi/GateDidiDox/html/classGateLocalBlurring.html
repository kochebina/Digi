Detailed Description

Pulse-processor for simulating a Gaussian local blurring on the energy spectrum.

  • GateLocalBlurring - by Marti.nosp@m.n.Re.nosp@m.y@epf.nosp@m.l.ch (nov 2002)

  • The user can choose a specific blurring for each crystals. Each time, he must choose the resolution and the energy of reference.
