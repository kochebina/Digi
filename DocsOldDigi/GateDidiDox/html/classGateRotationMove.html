Detailed Description

The GateRotationMove models a rotation movement

  • GateRotationMove - by Danie.nosp@m.l.St.nosp@m.rul@i.nosp@m.phe..nosp@m.unil..nosp@m.ch
  • The movement equation modeled by this class is theta(t) = V x t where theta(t) is the rotation angle around an axis at time t, and V is the angular rotation speed.
