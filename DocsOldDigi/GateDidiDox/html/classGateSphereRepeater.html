Detailed Description

The GateSphereRepeater models a repetition of an object along a spherical pattern.

  • GateSphereRepeater - by Delph.nosp@m.ine..nosp@m.Lazar.nosp@m.o@im.nosp@m.ed.ju.nosp@m.ssie.nosp@m.u.fr
  • The sphere repeater uses five parameters:
    • the numbers of repetitions along axial axis (Z by default) and azimuthal axis (Y by default)
  • the radius of the sphere
  • the angles alpha and beta
