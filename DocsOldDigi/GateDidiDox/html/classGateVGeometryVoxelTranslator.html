Detailed Description

This class is used to interpret the information in the digital images as material info.

  • GateVGeometryVoxelTranslator - by Giova.nosp@m.nni..nosp@m.Santi.nosp@m.n@ce.nosp@m.rn.ch
  • It has inside the rules to go from the image to the materials, on the base of funcions or tabulated information

  • The concrete translator classes have to implement at least the abstract method TranslateToMaterial
