Detailed Description

A G4VSolid which represents the region of a certain label (set of voxels which share that label) within an image. Used by the sub-volumes (GateImageRegionalizedSubVolume) of a GateImageRegionalizedVolume.

Inherits from G4Box : its dimensions are those of the bounding box of the GateImageRegionalizedSubVolume.
