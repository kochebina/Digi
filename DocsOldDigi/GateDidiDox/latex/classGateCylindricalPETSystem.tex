\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem}{Gate\+Cylindrical\+PETSystem}} models a Clear\+PET-\/like scanner. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem}{Gate\+Cylindrical\+PETSystem}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Daniel.Strul@iphe.unil.ch}{\texttt{ Daniel.\+Strul@iphe.\+unil.\+ch}}
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem}{Gate\+Cylindrical\+PETSystem}} models a scanner that obeys to the convention that were defined by the Clear\+PET software group. It comprises a hierarchy of components ranging from the rsectors (detector panels, arranged in several rings) down to the layers (individual crystals of a phoswich column).
\item This system overloads two methods of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateVSystem}{Gate\+VSystem}} base-\/class, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem_a15aa989d80915a4807972c19b6e2fb6f}{Describe()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem_a161957525535aba442ae54ed7f93f78c}{Print\+To\+Stream()}}
\item Beside the standard system methods, it also provides the method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateCylindricalPETSystem_abec178035e8f6b02baeb69a39ed549ed}{Compute\+Internal\+Radius()}} to compute the internal radius of the scanner 
\end{DoxyItemize}
