\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateArrayComponent}{Gate\+Array\+Component}} is a subtype of \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent}{Gate\+Box\+Component}}. 

It is meant to be connected to box inserters that are repeated

in arrays, such as crystal matrices
