\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOscTranslationMove}{Gate\+Osc\+Translation\+Move}} models an oscillating translation movement. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOscTranslationMove}{Gate\+Osc\+Translation\+Move}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Daniel.Strul@iphe.unil.ch}{\texttt{ Daniel.\+Strul@iphe.\+unil.\+ch}} (Aug. 10, 2002)
\item The movement equation modeled by this class is d\+M(t) = A x sin (2 PI f t + phi) where d\+M(t) is the translation vector at time t, A is the maximum displacement vector, f is the movement frequency, phi is the phase a t=0, and t is the time 
\end{DoxyItemize}
