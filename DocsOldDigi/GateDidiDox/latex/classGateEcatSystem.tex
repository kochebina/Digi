\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+System}} models a CTI ECAT family scanner. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+System}} -\/ by \href{mailto:jan@shfj.cea.fr}{\texttt{ jan@shfj.\+cea.\+fr}} and \href{mailto:comtat@ieee.org}{\texttt{ comtat@ieee.\+org}}
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+System}} models a scanner that obeys to the CTI ECAT family convention . It comprises a hierarchy of components ranging from the detector block (detector panels, arranged in several rings) and the crystals.
\item This system overloads two methods of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateVSystem}{Gate\+VSystem}} base-\/class, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem_a11818725a8dba26fcc84a4eab7482c66}{Describe()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem_a389f43cc9cbb875882d5f2c0de7ddf15}{Print\+To\+Stream()}}
\item Beside the standard system methods, it also provides the method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatSystem_ac19f79be397ef2f852b32b93eb4ee4fd}{Compute\+Internal\+Radius()}} to compute the internal radius of the scanner 
\end{DoxyItemize}
