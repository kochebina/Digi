\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent}{Gate\+Wedge\+Component}} is a subtype of \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}}. 

It is meant to be connected to Wedge creators, such as crystals or detectors


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent}{Gate\+Wedge\+Component}} -\/ by \href{mailto:rannou@informatica.usach.cl}{\texttt{ rannou@informatica.\+usach.\+cl}} (2007)
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent}{Gate\+Wedge\+Component}} is derived from \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}}, and thus inherits all the properties of system-\/components. In addition, it includes some methods that are specific to Wedge-\/creator creators\+: \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent_a1d7449ff82bfe624904a229307d182f0}{Cast\+To\+Wedge\+Creator()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent_aac69958680104367e251acf259809299}{Get\+Wedge\+Creator()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent_abb71e443877bd2fdbb4679ea58ba0690}{Get\+Wedge\+Length()}}
\item There is also a method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateWedgeComponent_a3fa25b1a54cdfce9bb6b58ebb0e80a9c}{Is\+Valid\+Attachment\+Request()}}, that overloads the method from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}} base-\/class\+: this overloading allows to check that the creator is indeed connected to a Wedge creator
\end{DoxyItemize}
