\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Pulse-\/processor modelling a simple PMT readout (maximum energy wins) of a crystal-\/block. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateReadout}{Gate\+Readout}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Daniel.Strul@iphe.unil.ch}{\texttt{ Daniel.\+Strul@iphe.\+unil.\+ch}}
\item The readout is parameterised by its \textquotesingle{}depth\textquotesingle{}\+: pulses will be summed up if their volume IDs are identical up to this depth. For instance, the default depth is 1\+: this means that pulses will be considered as taking place in a same block if the first two figures of their volume IDs are identical
