\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem}{Gate\+OPETSystem}} models the UCLA\textquotesingle{}s OPET system. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem}{Gate\+OPETSystem}} -\/ by Fernando R. Rannou
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem}{Gate\+OPETSystem}} models a scanner that obeys to the convention that were defined by the Clear\+PET software group. It comprises a hierarchy of components ranging from the rsectors (detector panels, arranged in several rings) down to the layers (individual crystals of a phoswich column).
\item This system overloads two methods of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateVSystem}{Gate\+VSystem}} base-\/class, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem_a977538a4a4d9012b1919cc2aa7aeb11f}{Describe()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem_ac6ee1ef69d2a44f4d7871381f21c6f71}{Print\+To\+Stream()}}
\item Beside the standard system methods, it also provides the method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateOPETSystem_a60991d06ed772995507bb6bb10c23e94}{Compute\+Internal\+Radius()}} to compute the internal radius of the scanner 
\end{DoxyItemize}
