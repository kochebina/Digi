\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classGateTrpd}{Gate\+Trpd}} creates a logical volume with a regular extruded Trapezoid (Trpd acronym) shape based on the GEANT4 boolean \char`\"{}subtraction\char`\"{} solid an with a box shaped extrusion. See Geant4 doc for further details. 

The definition of the Trd solidis based on 5 lengths \+: dx1,dx2,dz1,dz2,dz defining where \+:

dx1 \& dy1 give the length of the first X-\/Y rectangle crossing Z axe at -\/dz position

dy2 \& dy2 give the length of the first X-\/Y rectangle crossing Z axe at +dz position

The definition of the box shaped extruded volume is base on the 3 lengths/ the 3 coordinates of the box length/ center position. By extension, this boolean volume can also be used to create any shapes defined as a instersection with a \char`\"{}\+Trd\char`\"{} and a rectangle, like for example a simple prism.
