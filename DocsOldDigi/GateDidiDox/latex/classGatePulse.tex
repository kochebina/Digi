\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class for storing a \textquotesingle{}pulse\textquotesingle{} (luminous or electronic) derived from one or more hits. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGatePulse}{Gate\+Pulse}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Daniel.Strul@iphe.unil.ch}{\texttt{ Daniel.\+Strul@iphe.\+unil.\+ch}}
\item When hits are registered, theyr are converted into pulses by a \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateHitConvertor}{Gate\+Hit\+Convertor}}
\item These pulses may then be processed by a series of pulse-\/processors. The output of this processing is a pulse-\/list, which is then converted into digis.
