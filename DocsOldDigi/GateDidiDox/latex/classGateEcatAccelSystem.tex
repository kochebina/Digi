\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+Accel\+System}} models the CTI ECAT ACCEL scanner. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+Accel\+System}} -\/ by \href{mailto:delphine.lazaro@imed.jussieu.fr}{\texttt{ delphine.\+lazaro@imed.\+jussieu.\+fr}}
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem}{Gate\+Ecat\+Accel\+System}} models a scanner that obeys to the CTI ECAT ACCEL family . It comprises a hierarchy of components ranging from the detector block (detector panels, arranged in several rings) and the crystals.
\item This system overloads two methods of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateVSystem}{Gate\+VSystem}} base-\/class, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem_aed232a07e3ba697c692761000663dfe1}{Describe()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem_a28975b166e800ac89d7f9ce701a2881f}{Print\+To\+Stream()}}
\item Beside the standard system methods, it also provides the method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateEcatAccelSystem_a11c77f7758a3a7d2744b6d346ba20bb2}{Compute\+Internal\+Radius()}} to compute the internal radius of the scanner 
\end{DoxyItemize}
