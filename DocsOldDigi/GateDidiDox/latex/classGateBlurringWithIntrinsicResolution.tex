\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Pulse-\/processor for simulating a special and local \mbox{\hyperlink{classGaussian}{Gaussian}} blurring. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBlurringWithIntrinsicResolution}{Gate\+Blurring\+With\+Intrinsic\+Resolution}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Martin.Rey@epfl.ch}{\texttt{ Martin.\+Rey@epfl.\+ch}} (mai 2003)
\item Pulse-\/processor for simulating a \mbox{\hyperlink{classGaussian}{Gaussian}} local blurring on the energy spectrum (different for several cystals) based on model \+: \[R=\sqrt{\frac{1.1}{N_{ph}\cdot QE\cdot TE}\cdot 2.35^2+R_i^2}\] where $N_{ph}=LY\cdot E_{inputPulse}$. You have to give the intrinsic resolution (Ri) of each crystal. If you use also the modules for Light Yield, Transfert et Quantum Efficiencies (LY, TE and QE), this module takes these differents values, in other case they are equal to 1.
