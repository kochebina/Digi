\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent}{Gate\+Box\+Component}} is a subtype of \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}}. 

It is meant to be connected to box Creators, such as crystals or detectors


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent}{Gate\+Box\+Component}} -\/ by \href{mailto:Daniel.Strul@iphe.unil.ch}{\texttt{ Daniel.\+Strul@iphe.\+unil.\+ch}} (Oct 2002)
\item A \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent}{Gate\+Box\+Component}} is derived from \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}}, and thus inherits all the properties of system-\/components. In addition, it includes some methods that are specific to box-\/creator Creators\+: \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent_a4bb74f5a68a11b7446f3a69c72ac5aaf}{Cast\+To\+Box\+Creator()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent_a513e81e85588bbe5062e0d4463475c14}{Get\+Box\+Creator()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent_aa264264b8866f122c81113d8bfafc819}{Get\+Box\+Length()}}
\item There is also a method \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateBoxComponent_a846e143acd3b2f9771efbf4370ca6011}{Is\+Valid\+Attachment\+Request()}}, that overloads the method from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGateSystemComponent}{Gate\+System\+Component}} base-\/class\+: this overloading allows to check that the Creator is indeed connected to a box creator
\end{DoxyItemize}
